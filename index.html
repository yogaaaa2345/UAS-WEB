About COSRX

COSRX is a skincare brand from South Korea, widely recognized as a global innovator in beauty and skincare. Established in 2013, the name "COSRX" is a combination of two words: "Cosmetics" and "Prescription." This philosophy reflects COSRX's primary vision: providing effective solutions for various skin concerns through scientifically formulated products, using high-quality ingredients, and remaining friendly to sensitive skin.
